code coven

for ✨magical✨ black women and non-binary people who code

coven. noun.
  1. 1 : a collection of individuals with similar interests or activities
  2. 2 : an assembly or band of witches

Code Coven is a space for black non-binary and women developers to regularly come together and code. Modeled after sewing circles, Code Coven is an intimate, restorative, and productive community to share your resources, support, and works-in-progress.

All experience levels are welcome.
